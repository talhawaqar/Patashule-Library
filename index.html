How It Works

Patashule works in four simple steps

1-SEARCH:

It is very simple you can search the library by any keywork like name, place, city etc. Alternatively you can click on seach button on top navbar to set the list of all libraries and choose one by clickinng it

2-LOCATE:

Get to see where is library located by clicking on the library profile. It is even more exciting you can get the direction of library from anywhare.

3-APPLY:

Apply for the membership of the library of your choice. Click on the apply button to get the application form. We also follow up on the application to make sure you get reply on time

4-RELAX:

Once you applied for membership you can now take rest and visit our blog to see the best featured libraries of Pakistan and their detials.
